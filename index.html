📱 How to use AR on your Android phone
1
Make sure both files (building_ar_viewer.html and wissal_rami_v3.glb) are in the same folder on your phone
2
Open Chrome on your Android phone → tap the menu → Open file → select this HTML file
3
Tap the blue "View in AR" button above → point camera at a flat surface → your building appears!
4
Walk around it, pinch to scale it up or down, tap to place it
